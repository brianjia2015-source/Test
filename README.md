## ğŸ’¡ Game Concept: CATS Arena Reborn

### Core Mechanics
- **Build Phase**: Assemble car from parts
- **Enchantment System**: Apply up to 3 unique enchantments
- **Combat**: Drop into arena and battle

### Enchantments (PIT-style)
- **Lifesteal**: Heal on damage dealt
- **Fire Aspect**: Burn enemies over time
- **Damage Boost**: Increase base damage
- **Speed Boost**: Move faster
- **Shield**: Temporary damage reduction
- **Vampire**: Increased lifesteal at night
- **And more!**

### Progression
- Unlock new parts through gameplay
- Earn currency for upgrades
- Compete in ranked matches
- Seasonal content and events
